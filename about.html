About United Riders Charity Club

Ride. Give. Inspire.

Who We Are

United Riders Charity Club (URCC) is a Bohol-based motorcycle charity organization driven by purpose, compassion, and camaraderie. More than just a group of riders, we are a united movement committed to making a positive difference. Through organized charity rides, outreach activities, and fundraising efforts, we aim to support less fortunate individuals and families in need, empowering communities one ride at a time.

Our Mission

To create lasting social impact by uniting motorcycle riders in the spirit of charity, compassion, and community service. We strive to uplift the lives of the less fortunate through organized rides, relief operations, and outreach programs fueled by brotherhood and driven by purpose.
